Blueberry Muffins
M and M Rosich

Muffin Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups of flour
  • 3/4 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 tsps of baking powder
  • 1/3 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of fresh blueberries
  • Powder sugar

Topping Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of butter (cubed)
  • 1.5 tsps of cinnamon

Recipe:

  1. Combine flour, suagr, salt and baking powder
  2. Place vegetable oil in 1 cup measuring cup; add egg and enough milk to fill the one cup
  3. Mix this with flour mixture
  4. Fold in blueberries
  5. Make crumb topping by mixing flour, sugar, butter, and cinnamon with a fork
  6. Fill muffin cups with batter and top with crumb mixture
  7. Bake 20-25 minutes at 400 degrees

Blueberry Muffin History

Marissa started making these muffins a couple years ago and quickly became one of Mike's favorite breakfast treats.
