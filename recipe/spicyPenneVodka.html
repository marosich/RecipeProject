Spicy Penne Vodka
M and M Rosich

Ingredients:

  • Salt
  • 35oz can plum tomatoes (san marzano)
  • 1lb penne
  • 1/4 cup EVOO
  • 5 large garlic cloves
  • 4ox of cubed pancetta
  • crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 TBS butter
  • 3 TBS fresh parsley
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmagiano Reggiano

Recipe:

  1. Heat olive oil in the pot
  2. Add in pancetta and lightly brown
  3. Whack garlic with side of a knife and add in. Cook til lightly browned (about 3 minutes)
  4. Add tomatoes and bring to a boil. Season lightly with salt and generously with crushed red pepper. Boil two minutes
  5. Step 2Pour in the vodka, then lower to simmer until pasta is ready
  6. Just before pasta is ready, fish out the garlic cloves and add the cream and butter, stirring to incoporate
  7. Add the cooked penne and bring sauce back to a boil. Add more seasoning if needed
  8. Sprinkle with parsley and boil suance until it "clings" to the pasta
  9. Remove from heat and coat with cheese. Serve

Spicy Penne Vodka History

Marissa first started cooking this dish a couple years ago, and it quickly became a favorite. Now this has become a staple in their pasta rotation.
