Sesame Cookies
Biagioli

Ingredients:

    2 cups of flour ½ cup of sugar 1.5 tsps of baking powder ½ cup of butter 1 egg ¼ cup of milk 1 tsp of vanilla Sesame seeds

Recipe:

    Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in large bowl Cut in butter Stir in lightly beaten egg Add in milk and mix thoroughly to firm dough For 1 inch balls and coat with sesame seeds Place on greased cookie sheet and bake at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes

Sesame Cookies

This recipe comes from Marissa's Grandmother. They were one of her Grandfather's favorites, and to this day her mom still makes them on holidays and are enjoyed by the Mikes.
