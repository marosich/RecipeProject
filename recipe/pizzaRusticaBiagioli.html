Pizza Rustica
Biagioli

Filling Ingredients:

  • 2 sticks of semi dry sausage
  • 3/4 lb of sicilian salami
  • 1/2 lb of prosciutto
  • Fresh basket cheese (Formagetto)
  • 8 eggs
  • Pepper and grated cheese
  • 3 lbs of ricotta
  • Handfull of rice

Filling Recipe:

  1. Chop meat into small pieces
  2. Mix eggs pepper, grated cheese, ricotta and rice
  3. Add in chopped meat and basket cheese and mix

Dough Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of flour
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • 9 ybs of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1.5 cups of ice water

Dough Recipe:

  1. Lightly beat eggs
  2. Add in remaining ingredients
  3. Knead into dough
  4. Roll out dough in large lightly greased lasagna pan, covering bottom and sides (leave dough to cover top)
  5. Pour in mixed ingredients
  6. Cover top of lasagna tray with remaining dough
  7. Bake at 400 degrees for one hour

Strfula History

Pizza rustica is a traditional Easter dish. In the Biagioli family, they would typically eat it after the East Vigil for dinner. Each year Marissa's mother and aunt get together to make the dish together. Marissa's Grandfather would typically buy all of the ingredients for her mom the week before Easter.
