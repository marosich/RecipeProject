Pizzelle
Coppola

Ingredients:

  • 7 cups of flour
  • 1 /4 cups of baking powder
  • 1 /2 lb of shortening
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2 tsps of vanilla extract
  • 1 /4 cup of milk

Recipe:

  1. Mix ingredients in large bowl
  2. Roll into small balls
  3. Heat pizzelle iron and brush with oil
  4. Place one ball of dough in center and close iron
  5. Bake for 20-45 seconds or until steam is no longer coming out of the iron, then carefully remove from the iron

Pizzelle History

This fried dessert is tradidesigned dessert is simple, yet delicious. During Christmas time, Mike's Grandfather could regularly be found at the oven for hours on end making hundreds of these cookies, one at a time.
