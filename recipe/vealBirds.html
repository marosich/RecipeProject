Veal Birds
Coppola

Ingredients:

  • 8 thin veal cutlets
  • 8 slices of prosciutto
  • 2/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 8 anchovy filets
  • Flour
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 8 pimento-stuffed olives
  • 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe:

  1. Trim cutlets evenly
  2. Place a slice of prosciutto on top of each
  3. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese
  4. Place one filet of anchovy on each
  5. Roll and fasten securely with toothpicks or with thread
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Dredge rolls lightly in flour
  8. Heat butter and oil in skillet
  9. Saute rolls until nicely browned on all sides
  10. Add in wine and lemon juice, cover and cook over medium heat about 15 to 20 minutes
  11. Baste and turn frequently
  12. Five minutes before they are done, add in olives and parsley

Veal Birds History

Growing up, this was Mike's favorite birthday dinner.
