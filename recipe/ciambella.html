Ciambella Di San Biagio
Coppola

Ingredients:

  • 12 eggs
  • 12 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 lb of butter
  • 1.5 cups of sugar
  • Lemon zest
  • Cinnamon
  • Anise seed
  • Vanilla extract
  • 7.5 cups of flour
  • Amaretto

Recipe:

  1. Mix ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Let dough sit
  3. Shape dough into medium sized circles and placed on greased tray
  4. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes

Ciambella History

An "Italian Doughnut" this is a traditional dessert from the Abruzzo region. It is made every year at Christmas time.
