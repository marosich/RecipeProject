Pumpkin Bread
Borrell

Ingredients:

  • 3.5 cups of flour
  • 2 tsps soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp of baking powder
  • 3 cups of sugar
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 cup of cooking oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 16-ounce can of pumpkin
  • ½ cup of chopped walnuts
  • ¼ cup of water

Recipe:

  1. In large mixing bowl combine the flour, soda, salt, baking powder, sugar, cinnamon, and pumpkin pie spice
  2. In separate bowl, mix by hand oil, eggs, pumpkin, walnuts, and water
  3. Combine with dry ingredients
  4. Pour into loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour

Pumpkin Bread History

Marissa's family makes pumpkin bread around Thanksgiving. Typically they will have it as part of Thanksgiving morning breakfast.
