Farfalle
Coppola

Ingredients:

  • 12 eggs
  • 15 tbls of sugar
  • 15 tsps of oil
  • 2 tbls of baking powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Lemon juice
  • Orange zest
  • 8 cups of flour
  • Powder sugar

Recipe:

  1. Mix ingredients in bowl and let dough sit
  2. Roll out dough to be thin
  3. Using a pastry cutter, cut dough into small strips and pinch into middle (to look like a bow)
  4. Heat oil in pan, fry cut dough until golden brown
  5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar

Farfalle History

This fried dessert is traditionally made at Christmas time. It is a simple but delicious treat that has been made for generations.
