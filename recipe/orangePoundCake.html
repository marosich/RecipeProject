Orange Pound Cake
Borrell

Ingredients:

    1 cup of butter 2 cups of sugar 4 eggs Pinch of salt Grated orange rind 1.5 tsps of vanilla extract 1 cup of mil 3 cups of all purpose flour 2 tsps of baking powder 1 cup of sugar ¼ cup of butter ⅓ cup of orange juice

Recipe:

    Cream 1 cup of butter with 2 cups of sugar Add the eggs, salt, orange rind and vanilla Add in the milk, flour, and baking powder Pour into a greased 10-inch bundt pan Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour Dissolve 1 cup of sugar and ¼ cup of butter in orange juice and cook in pan for about 2 minutes Pour mixture over the hot cake and let it set until the cake is cool

Orange Pound Cake History

This comes from one of Marissa's mom's oldest cook books. It has become one of her go to dessert recipes.
