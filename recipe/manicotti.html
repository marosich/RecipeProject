Manicotti Borrell
Borrell

Crepe Ingredients:

  • 6 eggs
  • 1.5 cups of flour
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 1.5 cups of water

Crepe Recipe (makes 20-24):

  1. Mix crepe ingredients in blender
  2. Cook 3 tsps of batter at a time in non stick skillet - about 30 seconds on each side
  3. Stack crepes between waxed paper once cooked

Filling Ingredients:

  • 1 tbs of chopped parsley
  • 2 lbs of ricotta
  • 1/2 pound of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup of grating cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1/4 tsp of pepper

Assembly and Cooking Recipe:

  1. Mix filling ingredients in large bowlm
  2. Fill crepes with mixture, wrap, and place in greased baking tray
  3. Top with grated cheese and marinara sauce
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes

Manicotti Recipe History

This recipe comes from Marissa's mom. They typically make this for Christmas or New Year's Dinner as firt course.
