Seven Layer Cookies
Biagioli

Layer Ingredients:

    12 eggs 3 cups of sugar 3 cups of flour 6 sticks of melted margarine Grated orange rind 3 tsp of almond flavoring Yellow, red, and green food coloring

Layer Recipe:

    Beat 4 eggs and 1 cup of sugar Add in the yellow food coloring, orange rind, 1 cup of flour, and 1 tsp of almond flavoring Melt 2 cups of margarine and add in Pour mixture on greased tray and bake at 375 degrees for 10 minutes Let layer cool Repeat for red and green layer

Filling and Frosting Ingredients:

    24 oz of apricot jam 3 cups of chocolate chips 15 tsp of oil

Assembly Recipe:

    Put together layers with apricot jam Melt chocoalte chips together with oil Cover assembled layers with chocolate frosting and let cool Cut into squares

Seven Layer Cookies History

This recipe comes from Marissa's Grandmother. She would always make them on Christmas Eve, and were all of her cousin's favorite dessert. They would always sneak them from the cookie table during dinner. Now, Marissa has taken over the Seven Layer Cookie making duty, and they continue to be Christmas Eve favorites.
